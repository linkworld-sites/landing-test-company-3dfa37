Our Story

A Family Tradition, Elevated for Today

Three generations of leather craftsmanship shaped our founding vision: that a truly exceptional bag should be made slowly, deliberately, and with deep care for the person who will carry it.

The global handmade handbag market is growing fast — because discerning buyers know the difference between manufactured and made. We've built our entire identity around that distinction.
